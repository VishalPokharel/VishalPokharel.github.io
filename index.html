I am currently focused on building seoaiagent. Instead of just guessing what to build, we're actually running SEO for local businesses ourselves. Every time we find a task that works, we automate it. We’re essentially building the tool in real-time based on what we're doing by hand.

My goal is to develop AI agents that eventually outperform human-led SEO. I believe we can build agents that are genuinely smarter and more intelligent than a person when it comes to deciding the best possible action for a business's organic growth.

We are working toward a system where multiple agents handle competitor analysis, keyword research, content creation, and real-time optimization around the clock. We're starting with local services and will eventually expand to local e-commerce.
